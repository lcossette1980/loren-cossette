## What Is Chingona Clarity?

Chingona Clarity is a free AI-powered news platform built specifically for women in San Antonio. Every morning, it reads dozens of articles from trusted local and national sources, identifies the stories that matter most to you, and delivers a clear, balanced summary — no spin, no fluff, no agenda.

Think of it as your smartest friend who reads *all* the news and gives you the real talk over cafecito.

---

## How It Works

### 1. We Read Everything So You Don't Have To

Every day before you wake up, Chingona Clarity scans **11+ news sources** — from KSAT and the Express-News to the Texas Tribune, AP News, Reuters, and The 19th. We pull in hundreds of articles covering San Antonio, Texas politics, national news, women's policy, and community events.

### 2. AI Groups Related Stories Together

Our technology identifies when multiple outlets are covering the same story. A school board vote covered by KSAT, KENS 5, and the SA Report? Those get grouped together so you see **one clear story** instead of three confusing versions.

### 3. You Get the Full Picture

AI reads all the articles in each group and writes a single, clear summary that includes every perspective. If sources disagree, we tell you. If an important voice is missing, we flag it. You get the facts, the context, and what it means for San Antonio women — all in about two minutes of reading.

### 4. Every Story Gets a Bias Check

Before any story reaches your screen, it goes through an automated bias audit. Our system checks for loaded language, one-sided framing, missing perspectives, and unsubstantiated claims. Stories that don't pass get held back for human review. We take fairness seriously.
