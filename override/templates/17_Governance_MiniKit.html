Data Your Systems DO NOT Access
Example: Individual account records, PII, restricted/classified data, personnel files
3. Red Lines

What your AI systems will never do. State these unequivocally.

No autonomous decisions affecting personnel or employment
No access to restricted data without documented organizational approval
No outputs presented as final without human review
No use of data access as leverage in organizational disputes
No bypassing security protocols to prove a point
