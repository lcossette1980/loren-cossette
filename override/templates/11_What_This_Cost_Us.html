The “What This Cost Us” Retrospective
Documenting the Cost of Inaction After the Fact

This retrospective documents what the organization lost by delaying, blocking, or deprioritizing an AI initiative. It’s not a blame document—it’s an evidence-based record that makes the cost of future inaction harder to justify. Use after a delayed initiative eventually launches, or after a blocked initiative’s value becomes undeniable.
